UNDERSTANDING EU VAT & IOSS

What IOSS does for you

The Import One-Stop Shop (IOSS) lets you collect EU VAT at checkout on orders up to €150, then remit it through a single monthly return that covers all 27 member states. Parcels clear customs faster and your customers see no surprise fees.

Orders above €150 are subject to customs duty at import. We flag those, calculate the landed cost, and ship DDP where it makes sense.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Shipping to the EU, answered

What is IOSS and do I need it?+

The Import One-Stop Shop (IOSS) lets you collect EU VAT at checkout for orders up to €150, so parcels clear customs faster with no surprise fees. We can ship under your IOSS number.

What's the €150 threshold?+

Consignments valued at €150 or less are exempt from customs duty (VAT still applies via IOSS). Above €150, duty is assessed at import.

Do you ship to all 27 EU countries?+

Yes. We handle cross-border VAT and customs across all EU member states from a single integration.

How long does EU delivery take?+

Most Western European metros see 4–8 business days once an order leaves our dock, with tracking.
