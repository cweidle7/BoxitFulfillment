UNDERSTANDING UK VAT

The £135 rule, explained

The UK collects import VAT differently depending on order value. For consignments of £135 or less, VAT is charged at the point of sale: you collect it at checkout and remit it, and there's no duty. Above £135, VAT and any applicable customs duty are assessed when the parcel enters the UK.

We handle UK VAT registration, charge the right amount, file returns, and can ship DDP so your customers pay nothing extra on arrival.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Shipping to the UK, answered

Will my UK customers pay VAT on delivery?+

For orders of £135 or less, VAT is collected at checkout and nothing appears on delivery. Above £135, VAT and any duty are prepaid with our DDP option.

What is the £135 threshold?+

The UK charges import VAT at the point of sale for consignments valued at £135 or less; above that, VAT and potential duty are assessed at import.

Do you handle UK VAT registration?+

We guide you through UK VAT registration requirements and file the necessary declarations so you stay compliant.

How long does delivery to the UK take?+

Most UK addresses see 3–6 business days once an order leaves our dock, with full tracking.
