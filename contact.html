FREQUENTLY ASKED

Contacting Boxit, answered

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?+

We typically respond within 24 hours, often sooner during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM to 5 PM MST).

Do you have a minimum order volume?+

No strict minimum. We work with brands shipping a handful of orders a month up to several thousand.

Can I get one quote that covers multiple sales channels?+

Yes. Tell us every platform you sell on (Shopify, Amazon, eBay, and others) and we'll build a single quote that covers all of them.

Do you handle international shipping too?+

Yes, we ship to Canada, the UK, and the EU with duties and customs handled for you. See our International pages for details.

What do you need to put together an accurate quote?+

Your average monthly order volume, typical product dimensions and weight, and the platforms you sell on. The form above covers the basics, and we'll follow up if we need more.
